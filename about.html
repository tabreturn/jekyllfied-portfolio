Welcome to tabreturn.com -- a web portfolio of Tristan Bunn's work.

Tristan kicked-off his web design career back in the days of PlayStation 1, grunge music, and dial-up modems. Since then he's worked on a diverse range of cutting-edge digital projects including web campaigns for clients such as Samsung, BMW, and Mercedes-Benz; and more recently spent some time in the tech startup space.

Along the way he's developed into an accomplished coder and designer -- obtaining degrees in design and computer science along the way -- and is currently involved in lecturing, research, and work that blends code, interaction, interface design, and creativity.

He currently resides in Wellington, New Zealand. You can contact him via email:
portfolio@tabreturn.com
