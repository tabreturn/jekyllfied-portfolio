Welcome to the portfolio website of Tristan Bunn.

Tristan kicked-off his web design career back in the days of PlayStation 1, grunge music, and dial-up modems. Since then he's worked on a diverse range of cutting-edge digital projects including web campaigns for clients such as Samsung, BMW, and Mercedes-Benz; and more recently has been involved in various Cape Town based tech startups.

He's developed into an accomplished coder and designer -- obtaining a BTech in Graphic Design and Masters degree in Information Technology, in the process -- and is currently involved in lecturing, research, and front-end development work.
